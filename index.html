shopping list


Enter the item you want to add to your list into the text box.
Click the + button to add it to the list, or press the "Enter" key.

To select an item, click on it. Highlighted items turn this color.

To change the order of the list items, click the item you want to move and drag it to its new place on the list.

To remove an item, click on the white "x" next to the item.
